## HTML Text Formatting : 10 +6 = 16 types

- HTML contains several elements for defining text with a special meaning.

```html
<!-- 1 -->
<b> Bold text </b>

<!-- 2 -->
<strong>Kind of bold text</strong>

<!-- 3 -->
<i>italic text</i>

<!-- 4 -->
<em>kind of italic text</em>

<!-- 5 -->
<small>smaller text.</small>

<!-- 6. use for marking (or highlighting) the text -->
<p>used to <mark>higlight</mark> the text</p>

<!-- 7. use to show the text deleted-->
<p>used to <del>strike</del> the text</p>

<!-- 8. to show inserted text (will be shown as underlined)-->
<p>used to <ins>underline</ins> the text</p>

<!-- 9. Subscript text appears half a character below the normal line-->
<p>used to wite chemical formula H<sub>2</sub>0</p>

<!-- 10. superscript text appears half a character above the normal line-->
<p>used for <sup>foolnotes</sup></p>
```

## HTML Quotation and Citation Elements

```html
<!-- 1. to section that is quoted from another source  -->
<blockquote>From Vivekananda</blockquote>

<!-- 2. for writting something bettween " " -->
<q>between quotes</q>

<!-- 3. for writting abbreviations, when mouse over them gives full form -->
<abbr title="World Health Organization">WHO</abbr>

<!-- 4. for writting adderss in italic, and browsers will always add a line break before and after -->
<address>
  Jayrampur colliery<br />
  dhanbad<br />
  India
</address>

<!-- 5. for writting title of a creative work (e.g. a book), just writes in italic -->
<p><cite>The Scream</cite> by Edvard Munch. Painted in 1893.</p>

<!-- 6. Bi-Directional Override-->
<bdo dir="rtl">This text will be written from right to left</bdo>
o/p: This line will be written from right to left
```
